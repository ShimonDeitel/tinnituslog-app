Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-07

Ear Ring Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private Application Support directory. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics, no third-party trackers, and no network requests made by this app.

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit; we never see your payment information.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
